The Question ships six skills across two trees: two live in the plugin root, four staged in the 2026-06-21 session bundle.

## Live (the-question/the-question/skills/)

- **interrogate-spec** (planning) - stress-test a plan or design before implementation; grill/interview/interrogate a decision tree. Triggers: "grill me", "stress-test plan".
- **investigate** (engineering) - disciplined root-cause investigation for hard bugs and performance regressions, findings pinned to the board. Triggers: "diagnose this", "debug this", "broken".

## Staged (the-question-session-2026-06-21/skills/)

- **investigative-report** (writing) - turn raw material (research, notes, fragments, findings) into a sourced report or article; also shape/draft/restructure/tighten a draft.
- **faceless** (faceless mode) - terse, compressed, low-token responses. Re-voice of the retired caveman mode; legacy aliases "caveman mode", "use caveman".
- **faceless-commit** (faceless) - compressed commit messages. Legacy alias "/caveman-commit".
- **faceless-review** (faceless) - compressed code-review comments. Legacy alias "/caveman-review".
