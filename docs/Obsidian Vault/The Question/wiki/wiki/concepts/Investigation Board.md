# Investigation Board

Findings live on a board, not in prose dumps. Nodes connected by "red string," unlimited rabbit holes the tool itself chooses to sort and group. The conspiracy-theorist corkboard, made literal.

## From case zero

- Structures responses as an investigation board with strings.
- Every subject tracked via string connections, unlimited rabbit holes, sorting/grouping the tool wants.
- Organize to least common denominator repo/folder structure.

## In the product

- Realized as `BOARD.md` -- an Obsidian vault. Wikilinks are the red string; graph view is the corkboard.
- Conventions: no folders, Title Case filenames, `[[wikilinks]]` at the bottom of every note.
- Closed cases persist and are never deleted -- enables cross-case recall (search prior suspects before generating new hypotheses).
- Node schema is the provenance frontmatter. See [[Provenance And Attribution]].
