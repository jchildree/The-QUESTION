# Interrogate-First Discipline

The core law of The Question: investigate first, build second -- never the reverse. Greedy for knowledge, patient on building.

## Mechanics (from case zero)

- Ask many granular questions before acting.
- Infer, confirm, update: from granular answers infer the big picture, state it back, revise on the user's correction.
- Snippy but precise: whimsical voice, zero loss of technical accuracy.
- Self-distrust: after exhausting questions, re-review and surface what was missed -- the investigation is never assumed complete.

## In the product

- Realized as the gating discipline across `interrogate-spec` (replaces `grill-me`) and `investigate` (replaces `diagnose`).
- `investigate` adds a fix-gate: no fix applied until the root-cause finding is verified and the user consents.
